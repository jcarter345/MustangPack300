What a weekend! Pack 300 had an incredible time at our annual fall campout. Despite a chilly Saturday night, Scouts and families kept warm with campfires, hot cocoa, and plenty of campfire songs.

<!--more-->

## Highlights

**Hiking:** Dens hit the trails Saturday morning, earning some muddy boots and great memories. Several Scouts completed mileage requirements for their Hiking adventure.

**Campfire Cooking:** Saturday dinner was a pack-wide foil packet meal. Leaders were impressed by how the Scouts (and parents!) worked together to prep, cook, and clean up.

**Night Sky:** Clear skies gave us a perfect view of the Milky Way. Our Webelos den spotted several constellations and even caught a meteor!

**Service:** Before leaving Sunday, the pack gathered for a 30-minute trail cleanup, leaving the campsite better than we found it.

## Thank You

A huge thank you to our camping committee for organizing everything, and to every family who came out. Scouting is at its best in the great outdoors.
