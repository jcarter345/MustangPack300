Summer is almost here and Pack 300 is keeping the adventure going all season long! We have three great events planned — bring the whole family.

<!--more-->

## Summer 2026 Dates

### June 1 — Playground and Popsicles

Come hang out with your friends! Join us for a relaxed evening at the Good Shepherd UMC playground.

- **When:** Monday, June 1 · 6:30–7:30 PM
- **Where:** Good Shepherd UMC — playground behind the building, 10928 SW 15th St, Yukon, OK 73099
- **Activities:** Scavenger hunt, playground time, and popsicles
- **No RSVP needed** — just show up!

Wear play clothes and closed-toe shoes. Bring a water bottle. Questions? Contact John Carter (Den Leader) via band.

### July 6 — Stomp Rockets

We're launching into summer with stomp rockets! Scouts will build and blast off their own rockets.

- **When:** Monday, July 6 · 6:30–7:30 PM
- **Where:** Location coming soon

### August 3 — Recycle Raingutter Regatta

Close out the summer with a Recycle Raingutter Regatta! Scouts build boats from recycled materials and race them down the gutter.

- **When:** Monday, August 3 · 6:30–7:30 PM
- **Where:** Location coming soon
