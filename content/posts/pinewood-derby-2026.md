## Rules & Tips

- Cars must weigh **no more than 5 oz**
- Maximum dimensions: 7" long, 2¾" wide, 1⅜" tall
- Wheels and axles must be from the official kit
- All work should be done primarily by the **Scout** — parents are coaches, not builders!
