## New Scouting Year

We're thrilled to be starting another great year of Scouting! Whether your Scout is a returning member or brand new to Cub Scouts, we have an exciting year planned full of adventures, service projects, and fun.

### What to Expect This Year

- **Monthly pack meetings** with themed activities and advancement ceremonies
- **Den meetings** twice a month for focused rank advancement work
- **Fall camping trip** — details coming soon on the Events page
- **Pinewood Derby** in the spring — start sketching those car designs!
- **Community service projects** throughout the year

### Stay Connected

- Bookmark this site for news and updates
- Check the **Events** page and subscribe to our Google Calendar
- Questions? Email the Cubmaster at the address listed on the About page

We're looking forward to a fantastic year. **Do Your Best!**

— *The Pack 300 Leadership Team*
